Our History

Founded in 1975, Kou Fu Color Printing Corporation is a leading provider of high-quality packaging solutions in the Philippines. With decades of experience, we offer a wide range of products for both food packaging and industrial packaging ensuring strict adherence to quality, safety, and environmental standards.

At Kou Fu Color Printing, we believe in continuous innovation and investing in the latest technology to stay at the forefront of the packaging industry. Our state-of-the-art equipment enables us to produce high-quality packaging products while maintaining a commitment to sustainability and efficiency.
